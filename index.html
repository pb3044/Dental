About Our Clinic

Located steps from the Inner Harbour in Victoria, Harbour Dental Clinic offers preventive, restorative and cosmetic dentistry with a gentle, patient-first approach. We focus on comfortable visits, clear treatment plans, and same-week emergency care when possible.

  • New patient friendly — virtual forms
  • Direct billing available to major insurers
  • Accessible facility with street-level entrance

Our Services

Preventive Care

Cleanings, exams, digital x-rays, and oral hygiene education for all ages.

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings, crowns, bridges and implants to restore function and smile aesthetics.

Cosmetic & Whitening

Veneers, whitening, and cosmetic planning to help you smile with confidence.

Emergency Care

Same-week appointments for dental pain or trauma. Call our emergency line.

Children & Family

Gentle care for children: sealants, fluoride and preventive programs.

Oral Surgery & Extractions

Simple extractions and coordination for surgical referrals when required.
